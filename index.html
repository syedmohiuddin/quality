About Us

We, Quality Metal Industries, are the pioneering suppliers of electroplated components and extra fittings for two-wheelers across India. Through five decades of dedicated focus towards delivering the best and most optimal two-wheeler supplies for renowned brands like Enfield and Yamaha, we have established ourselves as a leader in this industry. We always make sure you get the best quality motorcycle supplies. This guarantee, coupled with our excellent customer service and attention to detail, ensures you get the most seamless experience purchasing our products.
