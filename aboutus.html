There are no secrets to success. It is the result of preparation, hard work, and learning from failure. With this determined vision in mind, Late Mr. Allah Bakash, the founder of Quality Group of Industries, worked all through his life. Despite his humble beginnings, Late Allah Bakash soon shot into prominence for his skills in Electroplating. Having ventured into manufacturing at a tender age, close to 45 years ago, he quickly adapted his skill-set to garner a specialized focus in the manufacture of extra fittings and electroplated components for motorcycles.

His brain child namely, M/s. QUALITY METAL INDUSTRIES,CHENNAI (INDIA), founded in 1967 became the pioneering supplier of electroplated components and extra fittings for two- wheelers all over India under the registered brand name of "Quality". Quality has established itself as a major player in the industry and as a leading source for electroplated two-wheeler accessories, especially for the Bullet and Yamaha brands. For more than four decades, Quality products have been leading the way to innovation and to new ideas. Its reputation for superior quality and prompt service has allowed it to create a system of excellent product development and efficient customer care.

Today, Quality has garnered an enviable reputation as compared to its competitors and is a hugely popular name in most Indian markets. The products are having an ever increasing demand in the length and breadth of the country, and have also reached overseas markets through our distributors and dealers.

As per our recent survey, Qualityâ€™s move to foreign shores has been welcomed wholeheartedly. Our dealers and distributors have been enjoying a very healthy and stable market thanks to the excellent popularity of our extra fittings and accessories.

After the demise of our founder, Mr. Allah Bakash, in the year 1996, the core management duties and business operations are being handled by his sons (Mr. A. Abdul Rafi, Mr. A. Riyaz Ahmed, Mr. A. Rizwan Ahmed). The company has been witnessing excellent growth under their leadership for the last decade.

In this rather niche Electroplating industry, Quality has been able to carve out a special place by virtue of adopting high quality work and skillful techniques. Apart from the phenomenal growth in sales, Quality has also ensured that the job labour in the Electroplating industry has seen a significantly upward trajectory.
